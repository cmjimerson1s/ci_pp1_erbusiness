What is an escape room?


Escape rooms are an activity that combines problem solving and team work into a fun, exciting, and exhilarating experience. You only have one hour to search a space, finding clues, riddles, keys, and locks, in an effort to solve obstacles and progress through the story.


Why pick our escape room as your next activity?


Improves teamwork and communication


Tests logical problem solving


Most importantly, it's fun!
